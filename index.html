Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

Do I need any experience to start? ▼
No experience is needed at all. We welcome complete beginners of all ages. Your tutor will assess your level in the first session and build a plan just for you.
How do the online sessions work? ▼
Sessions are conducted live via Zoom or Google Meet. You will receive a link before each session. All you need is a device with a camera and microphone.
Can I change my plan later? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Just contact us on WhatsApp and we will sort it out for you.
What is the money-back guarantee? ▼
If you complete the full 90-day programme and are not satisfied with your progress, we offer a full money-back guarantee. No questions asked.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes! Your first session is completely free. Book it through the Calendly link and experience a full lesson with one of our teachers before committing.
What ages do you teach? ▼
We teach all ages — from young children (5+) to adults. Our teachers adapt their style to suit every learner.

Ustadh Yassin

Lead Teacher & Director — MyMadrassa

Ustadh Yassin is a student of knowledge who took from the traditional school (maqra'a) of Morocco. Among his notable teachers is Shaykh Muhammad Abu Al-Yusr Al-Murrakuchi, a scholar who himself studied under eminent figures from Morocco, Saudi Arabia, and Mauritania.

In the field of Quranic sciences, Ustadh Yassin studied under Shaykh Waleef, who holds ijāzah in the Ten Qira'āt — both Sughrā (minor) and Kubrā (major). He also benefited from Shaykh Zakariyya Al-Andalusi, under whom he studied Usool Al-Fiqh, Nahw, Hadith, and Qira'āt. Eager to deepen his knowledge, he undertook several travels to meet and study under scholars in Kuwait and Saudi Arabia.

Ustadh Yassin is currently advancing his studies in the Seven Qira'āt through the method of Jam', and simultaneously studying three additional Qira'āt under a scholar from Algeria.

On the practical side, Ustadh Yassin has devoted nearly a decade to serving the Muslim community in the United Kingdom as an Imam and Khatib, and previously served as Imam for three years in France during his studies there.
